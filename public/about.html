About TradeCompare

Helping investors make smarter trading decisions through transparent broker comparison and financial insights.

Who We Are

We are a small team of passionate developers and finance enthusiasts dedicated to helping retail investors navigate the complex world of stock trading. Our goal is to provide clear, accurate, and unbiased comparisons of trading platforms available in the U.S. market.

Our Mission

TradeCompare was developed to simplify how traders evaluate online brokerage platforms. Instead of searching across multiple websites, investors can access structured, comparable data in one place.

Data Transparency

The platform focuses on presenting trading fees, supported markets, and performance indicators clearly while maintaining speed, usability, and accessibility.

Educational Purpose

TradeCompare provides a reliable and easy-to-understand comparison of U.S. stock trading platforms. We focus on presenting clear fee structures, account requirements, and platform features, so investors can make informed decisions quickly. This project also demonstrates modern web development techniques like responsive design, interactive tables, and performance-focused layouts.
